left shift operator
5<<1 (5 ko 1 bit left shift kar do)
ye generally multiply by 2 ho jata hai chote numbers ke liyea.
bade numbers ke liyea ye number ko -ve kar deta hai.
for example 010..0010 left shift 1 karne par ye Banega 10000..0100 (starting me 1 bit ho to negative hota hai).

Right Shift operator
5>>1 = 5/2
5>>2 = 5/2*2

Note: +ve numbers ke liyea 'padding' 0 hoti hai, for -ve numbers 'padding' is compiler dependent hoti hai
